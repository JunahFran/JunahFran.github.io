Basic Information

Amphibians are any animal in the class Amphibia that have thin, pourous skin, mucus-secreting glands, and an absence of claws. All amphibians belong to one of three orders in the group Lissamphibia, two of which in our range:

  • Frogs (order Anura)
  • Salamanders (order Urodela)
  • Caecilians (order Apoda) NOT IN OUR RANGE

Note: Amphibians contained in this site are only found in the United States and Canada. for more on amphibians all around the world, refer to the Wikipedia page:
